### Amazon Kinesis Data Streams
1. Go to https://console.aws.amazon.com/kinesis
2. Click on "Data streams" on the left hand panel
2. Select 'RetailDataStream' (or the name you chose) that you created for this workshop
3. Click on the 'Actions' drop down (top right corner) and click on 'Delete'

----

### Amazon Kinesis Data Analytics
1. Go to https://console.aws.amazon.com/kinesis
2. Click on "Analytics applications" 
3. Select 'RetailDataAnalytics' 
4. Click on the 'Actions' drop-down (top right corner) and click on 'Delete'
